About Interface Software Academy

24+ years of transforming aspiring developers into industry-ready professionals — our story, our mission, our promise.

📖 Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence

From a small training center to Odisha's most trusted software academy — here's how we got here.

December 2001

The Foundation

Mr. P.K. Dixit founded Interface Software as a software development & training centre in Bhubaneswar, Odisha with a vision to provide world-class tech education.

2005

Expanding Horizons

Introduced advanced training programs in Java, .NET, and database technologies. Student enrollment crossed 500+ annually.

2010

Software Development Wing

Launched custom software development services — building web and mobile applications for clients across India.

2018

Corporate Training Partnership

Partnered with leading organizations for corporate training and upskilling programs. Recognized as the best app development company in Odisha.

2024

Java + AI Revolution

Launched cutting-edge Java+AI program, integrating machine learning and artificial intelligence into our curriculum for the modern developer.

2025–Present

New Era, New Identity

Rebranded as Interface Software Academy with a premium, student-first approach. 10,000+ students trained and counting.

🎯 Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🚀

Our Mission

To empower students with deep, practical knowledge of Java and modern software technologies. We aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, ensuring every student who walks through our doors leaves as a confident, job-ready professional.

🔭

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted Java training institution — a place where innovation meets education. We envision a future where every aspiring developer has access to world-class mentorship and the skills to build technologies that shape tomorrow.

👨‍🏫 Our Leader

The Man Behind the Vision

👤

Mr. Prasanna Kumar Dixit

Founder & Managing Director

Mr. P.K. Dixit is a seasoned software engineer, educator, and entrepreneur with over 24 years of experience in software development and technology training. He founded Interface Software in December 2001 with a singular mission: to provide world-class, practical software training to students in Odisha.

Under his leadership, the academy has grown from a small training center to one of the most respected software training institutions in Eastern India. He has personally mentored thousands of students, many of whom now hold senior positions at top tech companies across India and abroad.

His areas of expertise include Core Java, Advanced Java, Spring Framework, Microservices Architecture, and AI-powered application development.

"A great developer is not someone who memorizes syntax — it's someone who can break down complex problems and build elegant solutions. That's what we teach here."
— Mr. P.K. Dixit
